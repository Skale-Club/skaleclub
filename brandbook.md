# Skale Club Brandbook

## Core Identity
Skale Club is a professional marketing service platform that emphasizes trust, quality, and ease of use.

## Color Palette
The brand uses a high-contrast blue and yellow scheme to project professionalism and energy.

- **Primary Blue**: `#1C53A3`
  - Used for hero backgrounds, headers, and primary branding elements.
  - Represents trust, reliability, and cleanliness.
- **Brand Yellow**: `#FFFF01`
  - Used for call-to-action (CTA) buttons, highlights, and important accents.
  - Must be used for all "Book Now" and "Instant Price" buttons.
  - Hover state should be a slightly darker shade: `#e6e600`.
- **Text Color**: `#1D1D1D` (Default)
- **Secondary Text**: Gray shades for descriptions and metadata.

## Typography
- **Display Font**: `Outfit` (sans-serif)
  - Used for headings (H1-H6) to provide a modern, tracking-tight look.
- **Body Font**: `Inter` (sans-serif)
  - Used for all body text, UI elements, and navigation for maximum readability.

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary CTA buttons must be Brand Yellow (`#FFFF01`) with black bold text.
  - Corner radius: `rounded-full` (pill shape) for main CTAs, `rounded-lg` for card-level buttons.
- **Cards**:
  - Soft shadows (`shadow-md` to `shadow-xl` on hover).
  - Subtle borders (`border-gray-100`).
  - Large corner radius (`rounded-2xl`).
- **Layout**:
  - Generous padding and whitespace.
  - Centralized filtering systems when subcategories are not present.

## Brand Voice
- Simple, everyday language.
- Professional yet accessible.
- Focus on the result: a "spotless space" and "peace of mind".
